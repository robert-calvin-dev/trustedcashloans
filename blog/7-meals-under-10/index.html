7 Meals for Under $10

Published by Trusted Cash Loans • May 29, 2025

Being broke doesn’t mean being hungry—or worse, living off ramen and regret. These 7 meals prove you can eat well, feel full, and still keep it under ten bucks.

1. Chili Mac

Boxed mac and cheese + a can of chili = one hell of a comfort bowl. Top with shredded cheese if you’ve got it. Feeds 2–3 people for under $7.

2. Fried Rice Remix

Day-old rice, frozen veggies, soy sauce, and an egg or two. Optional: chopped hot dogs or canned chicken. Costs under $5, tastes like takeout.

3. Bodega-Style Egg Sandwiches

Eggs, cheese slices, and bread or buns. Throw in some hot sauce or bacon bits if you’re fancy. Makes 4 sandwiches for about $6.

4. Bean & Cheese Burritos

Flour tortillas, refried beans, shredded cheese, and a splash of hot sauce. Toast ‘em in a pan for crispy edges. $8 feeds 3 people.

5. Tuna Pasta Salad

Rotini pasta, canned tuna, mayo, and frozen peas. Chill it or eat warm. Stretch it over multiple meals—around $7 total.

6. Ramen Upgrade Bowl

Start with instant noodles. Add a soft-boiled egg, green onions, leftover veggies, and maybe a splash of soy or sriracha. Gourmet on $3.

7. Budget Stir Fry

Frozen stir-fry mix + soy sauce + instant rice + one protein (tofu, egg, or chicken). One pan, full belly, about $9 all in.
