10 Great Financial Advice Blogs

Published by Trusted Cash Loans • May 29, 2025

There’s a lot of noise online. These 10 financial blogs actually deliver. Whether you're drowning in debt or planning your retirement, bookmark these and start reading daily.

1. Mr. Money Mustache

Frugal, fierce, and funny. Focuses on early retirement through badass money-saving tactics.

2. The Budgetnista

Tiffany Aliche makes personal finance digestible and powerful—especially for women and communities of color.

3. Financial Samurai

Deep dives into investment strategy, real estate, and financial independence from a former Wall Street pro.

4. The College Investor

Perfect for Millennials and Gen Z navigating debt, student loans, and side hustle growth.

5. Millennial Money

Grant Sabatier’s no-BS take on FIRE (Financial Independence, Retire Early), freedom, and financial mindset shifts.

6. Afford Anything

Paula Pant’s core idea: you can afford anything, but not everything. Prioritize and grow.

7. Get Rich Slowly

Timeless blog about steady wealth-building, not chasing hype. Consistent, realistic, and proven strategies.

8. Bitches Get Riches

Smart, sarcastic, and unapologetically real. Sharp money tips with swearing and sass.

9. Her First $100K

Built by Tori Dunlap, this blog focuses on financial feminism, salary negotiation, and confidence with cash.

10. JL Collins’ Stock Series

If you’re ready to understand index funds and why boring investing wins, this is gospel.
